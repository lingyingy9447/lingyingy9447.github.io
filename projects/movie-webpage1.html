Movie Webpage Project

Context/Content

The prompt of this project is to combine html and css to make an appealing webpage.

The theme of my webpage is a family being chase by a scarecrow at night. There are many mysteries that revolves around the plot, whether or not they escape or died during the middle of the journey is still to be uncover.

Reflection

One of the most challenging part of creating this webpage is meeting the requirement for padding. Padding is the space between the content and its border. I started by putting padding around the image, but I think it was kind of weird so I deleted the code. Next, I decided to put the padding around the input element but the padding isn't visble, making it meaningulness. Being stuck there for more than 10 mintues, I decided to turn to my partner and ask for his advice and we ended up putting the padding around the image element. Another challenge I faced is brainstorming about the project. My partner and I was totally confused on what to do for the project. We have no idea what the movie should be about, so we spended one period of class time to get the half of the planning and finished the rest at home. done.

My biggest takeaway from this project is communication. Teamwork make the things works. Through communication with my partner and other classmates, I was able to solve the problems that occurs throughout my project. My next step for this project is learn how to add the texts next to the images. The project looks kind of off with only the image on one line, it feels empty.
